### Key Ethical Questions in CS

| Area | Question to Ask |
|------|----------------|
| Privacy | Who has access to this data? Who should? |
| Security | What happens if this system is compromised? |
| Fairness | Who benefits from this system? Who is harmed? |
| Transparency | Can users understand how decisions are made? |
| Accountability | Who is responsible when something goes wrong? |
