### Measure Your Growth

> Measure your success by the **delta** between where you started and where you are now.

### Key Takeaways

1. **Problem solving** is the essence of computer science
2. **Input → Algorithm → Output** is the fundamental pattern
3. You learned **how to learn** programming — not just one language
4. Abstraction lets you build on others' work (like Scratch → C → Python)
5. Instructions can be high-level or low-level, specific or abstract
